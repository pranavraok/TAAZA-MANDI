Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a farmer?

You can register as a farmer by clicking on the "Register" button and selecting "Seller" as your role. Follow the simple registration process to get started.

How do I list my products?

After logging in as a seller, go to the "Post Upload" section where you can add your products with details, pricing, and images.

How do I buy/rent equipments?

Browse the marketplace as a seller, got to vehicle buy/rent page, select the products you want, and see if they are affordable.

How to access smart crop suggestor?

To access smart crop suggestor, log in as a seller. You must have an active subscription plan
