How TAAZA MANDI Works

A simple, three-step process at the heart of our e-commerce to create a fair and profitable marketplace for agricultural produce.

1

Farmers Post Their Products

Farmers can easily list their products with details and photos of their harvest, set prices, and manage their inventory.

2

Buyers Browse and Connect

Buyers search for specific produce, browse by location, and directly connect with farmers to negotiate and purchase.

3

Secure Transactions

We facilitate secure payments and help arrange logistics for a hassle-free transaction from farm to table.
